Transmission

As North America's No. 1 competitive transmission company, we're committed to providing our customers with the most innovative, cost-effective and reliable transmission solutions in the industry.

WE ARE DELIVERING THE POWER SYSTEM OF THE FUTURE, ONE PROJECT AT A TIME


NextEra Energy Transmission is the leading competitive transmission company in North America. We develop, finance, construct, operate and maintain transmission assets across the continent. NextEra Energy Transmission operates through its regional subsidiaries to integrate renewable energy and strengthen the electric grid.

NextEra Energy Transmission is an industry pioneer. Our subsidiaries were among the first non-incumbents to be awarded projects by system operators and utility commissions in California, New York, Texas and Ontario.

Our portfolio includes operating assets in California, New Hampshire and Texas, as well as numerous projects under development across the United States and Canada.

To learn more, visit www.NextEraEnergyTransmission.com.
